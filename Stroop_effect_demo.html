Stroop demo (mouse/touch responses)

Respond to the ink colour, not the word meaning. This version uses clickable buttons (works well on touch screens too). Reaction times will vary across devices.

Structure

  • 8 easy (congruent) trials
  • 8 hard (incongruent) trials
  • Mid-way summary after trial 8

How to respond

Click the button matching the ink colour.

Keyboard fallback is also enabled: R, G, B, Y.

Tip: For lecture use, you can mirror your screen and ask the class which button they’d click.
